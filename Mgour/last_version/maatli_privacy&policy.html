Privacy Policy

Last Updated on March 4, 2026

Maatli ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications (the "Apps").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, then please do not use our Apps.

We reserve the right to make changes to this Privacy Policy at any time and for any reason.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways depending on how you interact with our Apps.

A. Information You Provide to Us

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, username, and password.
  • Profile Information: Information you add to your profile, such as a profile picture or bio.
  • Communications: If you contact us for support or feedback, we collect your communications.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Data: We may collect device-specific information such as your hardware model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use our Apps, such as the features you interact with, the time and duration of your use, and in-app actions.
  • Log Data: Like most apps, we automatically collect certain log data (e.g., IP address, browser type, pages viewed) to analyze trends and administer the app.

C. Information from Other Sources

We may obtain information about you from third-party services if you choose to link, connect, or login to our Apps with a third-party service (e.g., Google, Facebook). The data we receive depends on your privacy settings with that service.

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our Apps.
  • Create and manage your account.
  • Process transactions and send related information (e.g., purchase confirmations).
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and support requests.
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities.
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues or fraudulent activity.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.

3. Will Your Information Be Shared?

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. We may share information in the following situations:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting, analytics, customer support). These vendors agree to protect your data.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or government agency).
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your information may be transferred.

4. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We will retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy, or as needed to provide the Apps to you. We will retain and use your information to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

5. Security of Your Information

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information. While we have taken reasonable steps to secure the personal information you provide to us, please be aware that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and Update: You can review and update your account information by logging into the app.
  • Delete: You can request deletion of your personal information by contacting us.
  • Opt-out: You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in those emails.
  • You can typically stop all collection of information by uninstalling the Apps.

7. Third-Party Services

Our Apps may contain links to third-party websites or services (e.g., payment processors, social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed to children under the age of 18 (or the applicable age of consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction. By using the Apps, you consent to this transfer.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
